Frequently asked questions

Common questions about the CBT portal

What is PrePa CBT Portal used for?

PrePa CBT Portal is used for computer based testing, exam practice, timed class-based assessments, and instant result reporting for students.

Which subjects can students take on the platform?

Students can take Mathematics, English, Civic Education, Basic Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, CRS, Government, Literature, Economics, Commerce, and other uploaded subjects on the platform.

Does the platform show results immediately after submission?

Yes. Once an exam is submitted, the system shows the student score, percentage, grade, and export options for reporting.
